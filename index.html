About Me

My name is Varish Adudodla and I am currently a Freshman attending Walnut Grove Highschool. I have a deep passion for Valuation and Corporate Finance, and I am always willing to learn more. The aim of this website is for me to share my DCFs and personal analyses of various companies. Each valuation will include an Excel Spreadsheet (DCF Model) and a supplementary Microsoft Word document. Much of my data and methodologies come from NYU professor Aswath Damodaran. I love keeping my feedback loop open, so if you have any questions please feel free to reach out to me at varish.adu@gmail.com.
